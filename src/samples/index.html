Pear Deck Identity

The number one question I get about Pear Deck is “Why the Pear?” The answer is that we wanted to create a unique brand in the K-12 education space filled with people who use words like assessment, differentiation, twenty-first century skills, and PowerPoint.

Pears are sweet, fun, and good for you. The brand strives to represent the mentality we hope to promote in K-12 classrooms.

See what I mean? Fun.
